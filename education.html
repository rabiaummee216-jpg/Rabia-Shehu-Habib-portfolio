Education

My educational journey began at Alburhan School, Kano, located on Old Police Station Street, Rijiyar Zaki. I completed my primary education there and sat for the Common Entrance Examination in 2018.

After completing primary school, I continued my junior secondary education at the same institution. Upon completing my junior secondary studies, I sat for the placement examination.

At that stage, my mother encouraged me to pursue science and technical education. I therefore sat for the Science and Technical Schools Entrance Examination and, by the grace of Allah, obtained an average score of 62%.

My success

Following my success in the examination, I gained admission into Mairo Tijjani Girls Science and Technical College, where I completed my senior secondary education from SS1 to SS3. During my studies, I developed a stronger interest in science and technology.

My achievements

In 2024, I sat for the NECO Senior School Certificate Examination and successfully obtained eight credits. In the same year, I also sat for the UTME (JAMB) examination and scored 216 marks.

These achievements marked the beginning of my journey toward higher education and a career in Computer Science. Today, I am pursuing my academic goals and working towards becoming a successful technology professional.
