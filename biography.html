Biography

I was born on 4th April 2005 and grew up in Rijiyar Zaki, Ubale Custom Street, Kano State. I am the first child of my mother and the fourth child in my family. My father has two wives, and together we are fifteen siblings, consisting of eight males and seven females.

From an early age, I combined both Western and Islamic education, a journey that continues to shape my character and values today. Education has played an important role in my life, helping me develop discipline, knowledge, and a sense of responsibility.

One of the most important lessons I learned as a child was the value of respecting and appreciating my parents.

  • Their sacrifices
  • hard work
  • endless support
have contributed greatly to the person I am today. I remain grateful for their guidance, care, and encouragement throughout my life.

My whorlds

My father has been a source of strength and inspiration to me. Whenever I faced difficulties or felt discouraged, his words and actions motivated me to keep moving forward and never give up.

My mother has always been my role model and a source of wisdom. Her advice, guidance, and unconditional love have helped me make important decisions and overcome challenges in life.

I also cherish my siblings, whose companionship, support, and shared experiences bring joy to my life. The strong bond we share has taught me the importance of family, unity, and cooperation.

Today☺️

Today, I strive to make my family proud by pursuing my education and working towards achieving my goals in the field of Computer Science.
