My Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy activities that inspire me, increase my knowledge, and provide relaxation. Some of my favorite hobbies include:

  • Listening to Qur'an Recitation: I enjoy listening to children recite the Holy Qur'an with proper Tajweed, beautiful pronunciation, and melodious voices. It brings me peace, motivation, and happiness.
  • Arabic Educational Nasheeds: I enjoy listening to Arabic educational and knowledge-based nasheeds that help me learn and appreciate the Arabic language and Islamic teachings.
  • Watching Animated Movies: I enjoy watching animated movies for entertainment, creativity, and the valuable lessons they often teach.
